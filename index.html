Dr. Gregory House

Gregory House, M.D. (almost universally referred to as House and rarely as Greg) is the main character and protagonist of the House series. He is described as a misanthrope, cynic, narcissist, and ill-tempered. As a doctor, his unorthodox diagnostic approaches and radicaltherapeutic motives created much conflicts between him and his colleagues and patients.
